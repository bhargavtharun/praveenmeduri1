Building Resilient Interfaces for food, water, and energy through Dynamic Energy conversion.

Welcome to the official page of the BRIDGE research group at Oklahoma State University. Our mission is to tackle critical global challenges at the intersection of the food-water-energy nexus.

We focus on designing and synthesizing advanced materials and developing innovative catalytic processes. Through a combination of experimental analysis and computational modeling, we aim to create sustainable and resilient solutions for a better future. Explore our site to learn more about our research focus, our dedicated team, and our latest discoveries.
